======================
Welcome to Mindboggle!
======================

We are developing Mindboggle to improve the accuracy, precision, and consistency
of automated labeling, feature extraction, and shape analysis of human brain imaging data,
and to promote open science by making all our `data`_, `software`_, `documentation`_,
and `publications`_ freely and openly available.
|
Please see our software `FAQ`_, `documentation`_, and `glossary`_ to get started.
|
